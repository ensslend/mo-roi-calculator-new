How we calculate your estimate

These figures are directional estimates based on aggregated data from Momentus customers, third-party industry research, and Momentus platform benchmarks. They are not a guarantee of results. Every venue is different — your actual impact will vary based on your team size, current processes, and how you deploy the platform.

Revenue Increase

Calculated as the number of revenue-generating events multiplied by a per-event revenue uplift rate, derived from observed improvements across Momentus customers in your venue category. Momentus customers have averaged 14% year-over-year revenue growth by reducing booking friction, capturing leads faster, and eliminating revenue leakage through better contract and billing management.

Operational Savings

Calculated as a per-event expense reduction across six operational processes: booking & forecasting, event planning, space utilization, event operations, accounting, and reporting.

Hours Saved

Time savings are estimated by summing per-process hour reductions across all events annually, plus approximately 2 overhead hours per event for coordination and communication tasks. Momentus customers have reported up to 83% reduction in time spent checking availability and responding to inquiries, and over 60% overall workload reduction at performing arts centers.

Data sources & assumptions
  • Revenue and expense uplift rates derived from Momentus customer survey data and platform usage analytics
  • Hourly savings benchmarked against industry research on venue management labor costs
